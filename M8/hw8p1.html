Analysis

This was surprisingly difficult to do without having to create an account and pay money for useful information. Despite that, I was able to use Google Trends to compare similar keywords in order to choose the best one. Semrush's Keyword Magic Tool gave a great overview particular related keywords by generating a list for you based on a keyword that you enter. The list has metrics attached to it, including the "Keyword Difficulty" which is how difficult they estimate it would be to be in the top ten search results for that search term on Google. It also included numbers for volume of searches of that keyword in a month and overall volume trend. Wordtracker provided similar information with a focus on volume and historical volume trends. I found that the list of related keywords was more relevant to my needs, but this can be pretty subjective.
